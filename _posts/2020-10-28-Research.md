My research concerns developing and applying high-dimensional cytometry technologies and analysis tools to map dynamic immune responses over time, space, and disease.

Contact information and links can be found on the
[about page](https://tomashhurst.github.io/about)

### Research and specialisation ###

**High-dimensional cytometry technologies**: high-dimensional flow cytometry, spectral cytometry, mass cytometry, Imaging Mass Cytometry (IMC).

**Computational analysis approaches**: Spectre analysis platform, time-series clustering using the ChronoClust algorithm, single cell segmentation and spatial analysis of IMC data.

**Application to disease**: viral encephalitis (West Nile virus, Dengue virus, Zika virus), systemic viral infection (Lymphocytic choriomeningitis virus, LCMV))
